Prescription Clarity Design System

A comprehensive design system for universal health tracking platform. Built for elderly users with accessibility and clarity as top priorities.

Accessibility First

WCAG AAA compliant with minimum 56px touch targets, 18px base font, and 7:1 contrast ratio.

Medical Grade

HIPAA & GDPR compliant design patterns with audit trails, data encryption, and role-based access.

Fully Responsive

Optimized for all devices from 320px to 2560px with progressive enhancement.
